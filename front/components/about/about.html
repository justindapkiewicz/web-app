Our Mission...

Financially support students who attend private parochial schools striving for excellence in the areas of academics and athletics. The foundation provides tuition grants that offer students an opportunity for unique depth and breadth to their educational and athletic experiences. In doing so, we hope students will graduate with academic distinction, leadership skills and distinguished moral principles.
